A comprehensive research project analyzing the viability of renewable energy sources in the Philippines through simulation and comparative analysis.

## Objectives

- Compare solar vs wind energy potential across Philippine regions
- Simulate energy generation under various conditions
- Provide data-driven recommendations for policy makers
- Support sustainable energy transition planning

## Methodology

- Geographic data analysis of renewable resources
- Weather pattern simulation for energy forecasting
- Cost-benefit analysis of different energy sources
- Environmental impact assessment

## Technical Tools

- Python for simulation and data processing
- Statistical analysis for viability assessment
- Data visualization for result presentation
- Geographic Information Systems (GIS) integration

## Impact

Contributes to informed decision-making for the Philippines' renewable energy future and supports national sustainability goals.
